The Strategy Behind It

Give a Little, Earn a Lot

Free Chapters Build Your Audience

Offer your first 3–5 chapters at no cost. This lets readers sample your writing without risk, building loyal fans who want more — and are willing to pay for it.

Paid Chapters Convert Fans to Buyers

Once hooked, readers unlock subsequent chapters using StoryVerse Coins. You set the price (e.g. 5–20 Coins per chapter). Each unlock = real money in your wallet.
